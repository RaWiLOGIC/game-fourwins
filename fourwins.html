TypeScript project game FourWins


FourWins

Rules of the game

The aim of the game is to create a row of four chips.

The row can be formed horizontally, vertically or diagonally.

The playing field is vertical. For this reason, the chips thrown in from above always fall on the lower free field or on the free field above an already thrown chip.

Info

The algorithm only calculates the next move. I think I've implemented a relatively good logic, but unfortunately it's still beatable.


Just play and try!
